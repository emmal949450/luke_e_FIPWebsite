About Paragraph

After years of working that 9 to 5 life, stuck in a gray lifeless cubicle, our founder Patty Prickles took a life changing trip to Mexico in which she spent some time at the Tehuacan-Cuicatlan Valley Biosphere. The peace and tranquility Patty felt while hiking and spending time appreciating the nearly 3000 species of cacti in the valley left her inspired. Shortly after returning from her trip Patty decided to quit her day job and Prickly Plants was born.

Our greenhouse is where it all begins, all of our cacti are grown from seed and nurtured to maturity in just the right temperature. Our small, close knit staff are passionate about plants and are all experts in growing the perfect cactus. They also love to chat and share their knowledge if asked and are always willing to give a complimentary greenhouse tour.
